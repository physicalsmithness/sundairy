What's happening

Consumer surplus is the gap between the most a buyer would pay and what they actually pay. Producer surplus is the gap between what a seller receives and the least they would accept. Drag the price line off equilibrium to see community surplus shrink.

Key idea. Community surplus (CS + PS) is at its maximum at the competitive market equilibrium. Any other price destroys value: some mutually beneficial trades simply stop happening. This is the allocative efficiency property of competitive markets.
